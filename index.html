Biography

Yingjie Cai is a Ph.D. student in Multi-Media Lab(MMLab) at CUHK, supervised by Professor Hongsheng Li and Professor Xiaogang Wang. She also works closely with Dr. Zhaoyang Huang, Dr. Sifei Liu, Dr. Xingyu Zeng and Dr. Kwan-Yee Lin. She got her bachelor’s degree in the CSE Department at Beihang University in 2018. Her research interests include but not limited to 3D Vision, especially on 3D Perception, Restoration and Generation.
