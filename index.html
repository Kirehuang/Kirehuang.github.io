Ph.D. Student

Biography

Kai Huang is a Ph.D. student in Management Science and Engineering from School of Economics and Management, Beihang University (BUAA, Beijing, China), supervised by Professor Guozhu Jia. He also works closely with Professor Wenyan Song at BUAA and Dr. Yang Cheng at Aalborg University. He got his master’s degree in School of Automation, Guangdong University of Technology in 2022, supervised by Professor Shengli Xie . His research interests include but not limited to Intelligent Manufacturing, especially on Fault Diagnosis, Multivariate Time Series Classification and Forecasting, Machine Learning.
